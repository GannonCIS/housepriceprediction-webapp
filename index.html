House Price prediction

Welcome to our property price prediction tool! This tool is designed to help you estimate the price of a property based on a few key factors.

Here's how to use it:

Step 1: Choose the location The first step is to choose the location of the property you're interested in. You can choose from one of four locations: Bangalore, Hydrabad, Mumbai, or Delhi.

Step 2: Enter the property details Next, you'll need to enter some details about the property. Specifically, you'll need to enter the following information:

Square footage: This is the total area of the property in square feet.

Number of bedrooms This is the total number of bedrooms in the property.

Resale status: Is the property new, or is it a resale? Select "New" or "Resale" accordingly.

Pool: Does the propert have a pool? Select "Yes" or "No" accordingly.

Jogging track: Does the property have a jogging track? Select "Yes" or "No" accordingly.

Step 3: Get your estimate
Once you've entered all the necessary information, simply click "Get Estimate" to get an estimate of the price of the propert based on the details you provided.
